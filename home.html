What is Afforestation?

Afforestation is the process of establishing a forest or stand of trees in an area where there was no previous tree cover. This involves planting trees in areas that haven't recently had any tree cover, in order to create a forest. The type of land planted could include areas that have turned into desert (through desertification), places that have long been used for grazing, disused agricultural fields, or industrial areas. Afforestation is an increasingly sought-after method to fight climate concerns, as it is known to increase the soil quality and organic carbon levels into the soil, avoiding desertification.
