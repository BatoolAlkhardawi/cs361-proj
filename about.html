Who are we

We are national Saudi volunteering group! We are a group of passionate individuals who are dedicated to making a difference in our community by reforesting the desert. Our mission is to plant trees and other vegetation in areas that have been affected by desertification, and we are always looking for more volunteers to join us in our efforts. By volunteering with us, you will have the chance to make a real impact on the environment and help combat climate change.

When was our group formed

Our national Saudi volunteering group was formed in 2015 by a group of passionate individuals who wanted to make a difference in our community. Since then, we have grown significantly and have been able to make a real impact on the environment by reforesting the desert. We are always looking for more volunteers to join us in our efforts, and we hope that you will consider joining our team!

Our goal to grow

At our national Saudi volunteering group, we believe that everyone has something valuable to contribute, regardless of their age. That's why we are committed to growing our group and including more people from different age groups. We believe that by working together and sharing our knowledge and experiences, we can make an even greater impact on the environment and our community. Whether you are young or old, we welcome you to join us in our efforts to reforest the desert and combat climate change. Thank you for considering joining our team!
